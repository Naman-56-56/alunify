Give Your Time and Expertise

There are many ways to volunteer your time and skills to support your alma mater and current students.

Event Support

Help organize and run alumni events, reunions, and campus activities.

  • Assist with event planning and logistics
  • Welcome and register attendees
  • Provide on-site support during events
  • Help with post-event follow-up

Alumni Ambassador

Represent your alma mater in your local community or industry.

  • Connect with prospective students
  • Organize local alumni gatherings
  • Represent the university at local events
  • Share university news and updates

Guest Speaker

Share your professional experience and insights with current students.

  • Classroom presentations
  • Career panel discussions
  • Workshop facilitation
  • Virtual or in-person speaking engagements

Alumni Board

Serve on the alumni association board or committee.

  • Help shape alumni programs and initiatives
  • Provide strategic guidance
  • Represent alumni interests
  • Attend regular board meetings

Share Your Knowledge and Experience

Guide current students and recent graduates as they navigate their academic and professional journeys.

One-on-One Mentoring

Build a meaningful relationship with a student or recent graduate in your field.

  • Regular meetings (virtual or in-person)
  • Career guidance and advice
  • Professional development support
  • Networking opportunities

Group Mentoring

Mentor a small group of students with similar interests or career goals.

  • Monthly group sessions
  • Industry insights and trends
  • Collaborative problem-solving
  • Peer learning opportunities

Flash Mentoring

Provide short-term, focused guidance on specific topics or challenges.

  • One-time or limited sessions
  • Specific skill development
  • Resume and interview preparation
  • Project-based guidance
